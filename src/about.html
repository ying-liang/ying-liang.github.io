Hi there! I'm Ying.

As an avid learner, I’ve always been curious about many things. Over the years, I’ve developed a passion for business, tech, and design. I’m interested in how they interact with each other. I think tech is the key to solving complicated problems, and business and design are the strategies behind it. I want to understand these fields and collaborate within them to deliver bigger, better results.

My background might look unfocused at first glance, but my education and experience has actually created a solid, yet adaptable foundation. I have a breadth of skills, and now I’m building depth. In my startup roles, I wore many hats. I was exposed to different business functions and department-org interactions. I developed brand, content, and strategy which helped me contextualize my work. When I started work in software development and UX consulting, I built hard skills with industry-specific tools that reinforced the principles I learned earlier.

My unique selling points come from my diverse background. I can communicate and work with a variety of stakeholders, from business people to tech people to creatives. I’m able to build user-centric products, and communicate the business value of design. I can see the big picture across disciplines and also be detail oriented on the ground level. With 3 years of work experience, I've built a foundation for business acumen, soft skills, and client communication.

All of my work revolves around empathetic design. I want to understand a user’s story and help people do more, better. As a consumer, I'm drawn to seamless, intuitive user interfaces. I truly believe a delightful user experience can help a brand foster competitive advantage, positively impacting the lives of others.
