Hi there! I'm Ying.

I’m a mid-20s-something living in the Midwest (USA), still trying to figure out what I want to be. All I know is I have a passion for people and tech, and I want to explore new ways to connect the two!

For the past few years I’ve worked in tech startups. I’ve had to wear many hats and learn how to interact with different stakeholders: from developers to business analysts, from sales to marketing professionals, from customers/clients big and small. My previous full-time role was in brand and content management at a B2B SaaS company, where I learned a great deal about my values and work style.

During my undergrad, I studied media, psychology, business, and writing. At first glance they may seem like divergent fields, but they can be distilled into two main areas of interest: human behavior and communication. I believe my experience in these disciplines come together to make me a well-rounded team member who’s better able to collaborate and drive strategic business growth.

That’s the reason I’m interested in UX. It's a unique field that requires design thinking and empathy. I want to understand a user’s story and help people do more, better. As a consumer, I begrudge inconsistent, buggy experience and applaud interfaces that are seamless and intuitive. I truly believe a delightful user experience can help a brand foster competitive advantage, positively impacting the lives of others.

As I’m pursuing a lateral career change, I’m adding more tools to my toolbox with each forward step I take on my journey. I look forward to developing my craft and hope I get a chance to work with you. Thanks for stopping by and have a great day.
